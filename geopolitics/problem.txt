In the past 48 hours, satellite intelligence detected a massive concentration of Rival State troops along the border of the demilitarized zone, accompanied by a series of simultaneous cyberattacks on our national power grid. We know that if the Rival State is planning an imminent, large-scale invasion against us, it mobilizes troops at the border. Consequently, the Chief of Defense Staff proposes to immediately activate a Class-A general mobilization state, recalling 50,000 reservists and deploying the entire naval fleet to the border waters—an emergency logistical operation that will cost the country €120 million in extraordinary budget.
