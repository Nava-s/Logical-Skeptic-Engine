**P1 INCIDENT DIRECTIVE — Evidence Before Code**

Fifteen years of incident response taught me one law: more money is burned by confident guesses than by any outage. Here is what is CONFIRMED: a 300% cost spike, elevated invocations and duration. Here is what is NOT: the infinite-loop theory — nobody has read a single trace. I will not sign a code change against an unverified diagnosis, and neither should you.

1. CloudWatch logs and X-Ray traces pulled within the hour; identify the actual driver.
2. A targeted fix follows the evidence — not the standup speculation.

Anyone pushing a patch before the traces are read is gambling with production and calling it decisiveness.
