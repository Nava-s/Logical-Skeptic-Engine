**SEV-1 DIRECTIVE — Isolate, Then Revert**

A hundred releases taught me the most expensive Sev-1 mistake: rolling back the wrong change and burning the outage window twice. CONFIRMED: p99 at 5x since a deploy that bundled TWO changes — a migration and a cache layer. NOT confirmed: which one is responsible, because nobody has isolated anything yet.

1. Cache feature-flag off behind a canary within the hour; watch p99.
2. Slow-query logs reviewed in parallel for migration impact.
3. We revert exactly what the data implicates — nothing else.

Isolation is not hesitation. It is the difference between one incident and two.
