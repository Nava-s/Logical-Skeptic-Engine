**NSC BRIEF — Posture Follows Corroboration**

Three decades of intelligence work taught me one law that has never been repealed: posture follows corroboration. CONFIRMED: a border troop concentration and cyberattacks on civilian infrastructure. NOT confirmed: intent, and attribution of the attacks. Mobilizing against an inference is how miscalculations become wars.

1. All-source tasking — HUMINT, SIGINT, allied liaison — intent assessment in 24h.
2. Back-channel opened to test for a diplomatic readout.
3. Every mobilization decision holds pending corroboration.

This is not patience as a virtue. It is professional discipline as a survival trait.
