Over the past 72 hours, IoT sensors mounted on precision agriculture drones detected a drastic 30% drop in the Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) combined with anomalous water stress in the North quadrant of our premium vineyard (a Barolo DOCG). We know that if the lethal downy mildew fungus attacks the plants destructively, the NDVI index immediately drops by 30% and roots show acute water stress. Consequently, the Head Agronomist proposes to immediately purchase an emergency specialized chemical treatment imported from abroad costing €45,000 to be sprayed across the entire estate within 24 hours to save the harvest.
